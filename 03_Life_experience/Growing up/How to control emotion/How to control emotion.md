# How to control emotion
- Angry -> Hit The Gym
- Uninspired -> Take a shower
- Anxious -> Take deep breaths
- Sad -> Reflect on past victories
- Irritated -> Go on long walks
- Tired -> Go to bed
- Doubtful -> Remember why you started
