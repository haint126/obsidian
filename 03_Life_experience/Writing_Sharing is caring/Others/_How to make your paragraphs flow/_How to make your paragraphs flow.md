# How to make your paragraphs flow:
## Add details
- Also
- In addition
- In other words

## Add reasons
- So
- Since
- That's why

## Add exception
- But
- However
- Alternatively

## Add contrast
- While
- Whereas
- On the other hand

## Add examples
- Like
- Such as
- Including

## Add persuasion:
- Certainly
- Absolutely
- Definitely

## Add expansion:
- Moreover
- Furthermore
- Similarly
